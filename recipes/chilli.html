Chilli Spice Mix

This chilli spice mix goes well with just about any type of chilli, be careful though it does pack a small punch

These are all the spices you'll need

Ingredients

  • 3 tbsp chili powder
  • 1 tbsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp red pepper flakes
  • 1/4 cayenne
  • 1/4 tsp ground cinnamon

Steps

  1. Mix all ingredients together in a small bowl
  2. Once your meat is browned and your veg is sweated stir in the spice mix and let fry for 1-2 mins untill fragrant
  3. Add your wet ingredients and cook down
